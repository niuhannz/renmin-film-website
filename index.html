以影像探索人性

人民電影有限公司成立於台灣，是一家專注於劇情長片、紀錄片及實驗動畫的獨立製片公司。我們致力於發掘深刻的人文故事，以獨特的影像語言呈現台灣及亞洲的多元面貌。

PEOPLE FILM CO., LTD is a Taiwanese independent production company specializing in narrative features, documentaries, and experimental animation. We are dedicated to uncovering profound human stories, presenting the diverse faces of Taiwan and Asia through a distinctive cinematic language.

從前期開發到後期製作，我們的團隊以嚴謹的態度和充沛的創造力，打造每一部觸動人心的作品。

From development to post-production, our team approaches each project with rigor and abundant creativity, crafting works that move the heart.

The People Behind the Films

The People Behind the Films

影像背後的人

我們的團隊匯聚了來自台灣及國際的優秀電影與動畫創作者。每位成員都帶著對影像藝術的熱情與獨特的創作視野，共同打造觸動人心的作品。 Our team brings together outstanding filmmakers and animators from Taiwan and around the world. Each member carries a passion for the art of cinema and a unique creative vision, collaborating to craft works that move the heart.

人民電影由一群熱愛影像創作的夥伴共同組成。團隊成員橫跨導演、製片、動畫、攝影、美術及後期製作等領域，擁有豐富的實戰經驗與國際合作背景。

PEOPLE FILM is composed of a group of passionate visual storytellers. Our team spans directing, producing, animation, cinematography, art direction, and post-production, bringing rich hands-on experience and an international collaborative background.

我們相信好的故事來自多元的視角。團隊中有深耕紀錄片的資深導演、專注實驗動畫的創作者、擅長光影敘事的攝影師，以及串連每一個環節的製片人。正是這樣不同背景的碰撞與交融，讓我們的作品能跨越語言與文化，觸及更廣闊的觀眾。

We believe great stories come from diverse perspectives. Our team includes veteran documentary directors, experimental animators, cinematographers skilled in visual narrative, and producers who connect every link in the chain. It is precisely this collision and fusion of different backgrounds that allows our work to transcend language and culture, reaching a wider audience.
