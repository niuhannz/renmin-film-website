導演A film by — Niu Han 牛涵

劇情簡介Synopsis

在台北的家庭教會中，虔誠的雅各深陷破碎的婚姻，卻在此時愛上了一位寡婦。他以「唯獨聖經」的偏執，為自己的激情找到了聖經中一夫多妻的依據。

In a Taipei home church, Jacob, a devout man in a troubled marriage, falls for a widow. Guided by a "sola-scriptura" obsession, he justifies his passion as biblically-endorsed polygamy.

這種古老的邏輯衝擊了他的現代家庭、事業與信仰社群。被驅逐、被羞辱之後，雅各仍然緊抱著他獨特的義——在一個墮落的世界中，選擇自己的「誡命」。

This archaic reasoning shatters his modern family, business, and faith community. Expelled and humiliated, Jacob clings to his distinctive righteousness, choosing his own "commandment" in a fallen world.
